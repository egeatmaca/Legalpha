About

What is Legalpha?

Legalpha is a chatbot that answers questions about tenancy law in easy to understand plain English.
It is built by Legalpha Community on a voluntary basis and free for everyone.

How did it start?

For equal rights, everyone should have equal access to legal information.
However, legal information is often hard to understand and not easily accessible.
This results in inequalities between people who can afford a lawyer and people who can't.
That was exactly my situation, when I had a conflict with a big real estate company with many lawyers.
I could have paid them lots of money, just because I didn't know my rights properly.
Although it was a though journey, I stood up, researched a lot and finally convinced them, I was right.
Today, the purpose of Legalpha is democratizing legal system by making legal information more accessible and understandable for more people like me.
